Aim

To study partial differentiation of composite functions using the chain rule and verify the results numerically.

Theory

If f = f(u,v), where u = g(x,y) and v = h(x,y), then:

df/dx = df/du · du/dx + df/dv · dv/dx
df/dy = df/du · du/dy + df/dv · dv/dy

Pretest

1. What is the chain rule for two independent variables?

2. If u = x² + y, what is du/dx?
